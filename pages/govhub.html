GovHub Case Study

UI/UX Design • Mobile App

GovHub is a mobile application designed to simplify access to government services. The goal was to create a clean, user-friendly interface that helps users easily find, apply, and track services without confusion.

Problem

Users often struggle with complex government portals, unclear navigation, and lack of transparency in service processes.

Solution

Designed a simplified mobile experience with clear navigation, service categories, and step-by-step guidance to improve usability and accessibility.
